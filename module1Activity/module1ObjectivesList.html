List of objectives for Module 1 - Real and Complex Numbers.

In this homework, we will review categories of numbers and the properties these numbers have. The objectives for this homework are:
(a)
Identify the subgroup of Real numbers a number belongs to.
(b)
Identify the subgroup of Complex numbers a number belongs to.
(c)
Apply the properties of Real numbers to simplify expressions.
(d)
Add, Subtract, Multiply, or Divide Complex numbers.
